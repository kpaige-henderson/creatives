About Motif.MoveMINT

Motif.MoveMINT blends the art of dance, the craft of sound, and the eye for design into one innovative studio. We believe in the power of creativity to transform ideas into experiences that inspire. Our work is rooted in movement, whether it’s through the grace of dance, the energy of music, or the creativity of reimagining spaces.

At Motif.MoveMINT, we specialize in choreography that moves audiences, music editing that sets the perfect tone, and furniture flipping that breathes new life into forgotten pieces. Our approach is holistic, combining these passions to create a studio that not only moves bodies and minds but also revitalizes the spaces around us.

Whether you’re here to enhance your next performance, discover new sounds, or transform a piece of furniture into a masterpiece, Motif.MoveMINT is where artistry and craftsmanship come together to make every project a work of art.
